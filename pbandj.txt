Go to C-town
Pick up shopping basket
If store has bread, put bread into shopping basket
If store has peanut butter, put peanut butter into shopping basket
If store has jelly, put jelly into shopping basket
Go to checkout
Pay for whatever was purchased
If pb, jelly, or bread are missing in your bag, then go to Natural's
Repeat steps 3-7
If pb, jelly, or bread are missing in your bag, then forget about making pb + j sandwich
Come back home
Open bread plastic bag by tearing it apart
Open jelly container by twisting the top clockwise
Open peanut butter container by twisting the top clockwise
Take out 2 pieces of bread
Lie the 2 pieces of bread flat
Take out a spreading knife from drawer
If no knife, then use spoon
If no spoon, then use fork
If no fork, then forget about making pb + j sandwich
Take some peanut butter
Spread .3 inches of the peanut butter on one piece of bread
Take some jelly
Spread .3 inches of the jelly on the other piece of bread
Put the 2 pieces of bread together with pb + j sides facing eacher other
Close peanut butter container by twisting top counter clockwise
Close jelly container by twisting top counter clockwise
Close bread container
Put pb and j sandwich on plate
If no plate, then put it on a napkin
If no napkin, then just hold it in your hands
Eat pb and j sandwich
